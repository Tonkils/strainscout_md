# Strategic Document 2: Instagram Content & Social Media Marketing Strategy

**Author:** Manus AI
**Date:** March 26, 2026

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the Instagram marketing strategy for StrainScoutMD, designed to be executed by Emily. The goal is to build brand awareness, drive traffic to the website, and capture the surge in consumer interest leading up to the 4/20 holiday. The strategy centers on an aggressive 5x/day posting schedule, carefully balancing promotional content with educational value while strictly adhering to Instagram's community guidelines regarding cannabis content.

## 2. Platform Constraints and Compliance
Instagram strictly prohibits the sale of cannabis and often flags accounts that appear to promote consumption. To avoid account suspension—a critical risk during a launch phase—all content must adhere to the following guidelines:
- **No Direct Sales:** Never use terms like "buy," "sell," "price," or "discount" in the imagery or captions. Use coded language like "find the best value" or "compare options."
- **No Consumption Imagery:** Do not show people smoking, vaping, or consuming cannabis.
- **Educational Focus:** Frame the platform as an educational tool for medical patients and adult-use consumers to make informed decisions based on data (terpenes, THC, genetics).
- **Link in Bio:** Direct all traffic to the link in the bio; do not attempt to post clickable links in captions or comments.

## 3. The 5x/Day Posting Strategy
To maximize reach and algorithmic favorability leading up to 4/20, Emily will execute a high-frequency posting schedule. This requires a mix of content formats (Reels, Stories, Carousels, and Static Posts) to maintain engagement without causing audience fatigue.

### 3.1 Daily Content Pillars
The 5 daily posts should be distributed across the following content pillars:

1. **The Daily Drop (Morning - Story/Static):** Highlight a specific strain that has seen a significant price decrease across Maryland dispensaries. Focus on the data: "Strain X is showing high availability today. Check the link in bio to compare."
2. **Terpene Tuesday/Education (Mid-day - Carousel):** Educational content explaining the effects of specific terpenes (e.g., "Why Myrcene helps you sleep"). This builds authority and aligns with StrainScoutMD's detailed data tracking.
3. **Dispensary Spotlight (Afternoon - Reel):** A quick, fast-paced video highlighting a specific region in Maryland (e.g., "Top 3 spots to check out in Baltimore County").
4. **User Journey/Platform Feature (Evening - Reel/Story):** A screen-recording tutorial showing how easy it is to use StrainScoutMD to filter by "Cheapest" or set a "Price Alert."
5. **Community/Meme (Night - Static):** Relatable, Maryland-specific cannabis culture content to drive shares and saves.

### 3.2 The 4/20 Countdown Campaign
Starting three weeks prior to April 20th, the messaging will shift to a countdown format.

- **Weeks 1-2 (Awareness):** "Don't overpay this 4/20. Start tracking your favorite strains now." Encourage users to sign up for the weekly email newsletter to get early access to holiday deals.
- **Week 3 (Urgency):** "The 4/20 menus are dropping. We're tracking 66 dispensaries so you don't have to." Increase the frequency of Stories showing real-time price comparisons.
- **April 19-20 (Execution):** Shift entirely to real-time updates via Instagram Stories, directing users to the "Cheapest" and "Top Value" pages on the site.

## 4. Content Creation Workflow for Emily
To sustain a 5x/day schedule, Emily must batch-create content.

1. **Data Mining (Monday):** Use StrainScoutMD's backend data to identify the week's top strains, biggest price drops, and most popular dispensaries.
2. **Asset Creation (Tuesday/Wednesday):** Design static graphics and carousels using templates (e.g., Canva). Record screen-capture tutorials for Reels.
3. **Scheduling (Thursday):** Use a social media management tool (e.g., Later, Buffer) to schedule the static posts and carousels. Reels and Stories should be posted natively to maximize reach.

## 6. Conclusion
By executing this high-frequency, compliance-aware Instagram strategy, StrainScoutMD will rapidly build an audience of price-conscious Maryland cannabis consumers. The focus on data, education, and platform utility will differentiate the brand from dispensaries and position it as an indispensable tool for the 4/20 holiday and beyond.
