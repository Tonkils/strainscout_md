# StrainScoutMD: Comprehensive Research Plan

**Author:** Manus AI
**Date:** March 26, 2026

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the structured research methodology required to determine the optimal launch strategy for StrainScoutMD.com. The primary objective is to understand the competitive landscape, target audience behaviors, and market dynamics within the Maryland cannabis sector. By executing this plan, the team will gather actionable insights to position StrainScoutMD as the premier price comparison and deal-finding platform in the state, ensuring a successful launch prior to the critical 4/20 retail period.

## 2. Research Objectives
The research initiative is driven by three core objectives designed to inform the platform's strategic direction. First, the team must analyze the competitive environment to identify gaps in existing solutions like Leafly and Weedmaps, specifically focusing on their limitations in cross-dispensary price aggregation. Second, understanding consumer behavior is paramount; the research will uncover how Maryland cannabis consumers currently discover deals, their price sensitivity, and their preferred communication channels. Finally, the research will evaluate the technical and marketing readiness of StrainScoutMD, ensuring the platform meets user expectations for data accuracy and usability.

## 3. Methodology and Approach

### 3.1 Competitive Analysis
The competitive analysis will involve a deep dive into both national platforms and local Maryland resources. The team will evaluate the feature sets, user interfaces, and data freshness of competitors. A key focus will be assessing how these platforms handle price comparisons and whether they offer true cross-dispensary aggregation or merely single-dispensary menus. This phase will also include an audit of competitor marketing strategies, particularly their approach to the 4/20 holiday and their presence on social media platforms like Instagram.

### 3.2 Consumer Behavior and Market Sizing
To understand the target audience, the research will leverage secondary data sources, including state-published cannabis sales reports and industry analyses. The Maryland cannabis market is currently annualizing at over $1.2 billion, with flower remaining the dominant product category [1]. The research will analyze purchasing trends, average basket sizes, and the demographic profile of the Maryland cannabis consumer. Additionally, social listening on platforms like Reddit (e.g., r/MDEnts) will provide qualitative insights into consumer pain points regarding price transparency and deal discovery [2].

### 3.3 Technical and UX Evaluation
A critical component of the research involves a thorough evaluation of the StrainScoutMD platform itself. This includes mapping the user journey from landing page to product discovery and outbound click to a dispensary website. The research will identify any technical friction points, such as the currently observed 403 Forbidden errors on key subpages, and assess the effectiveness of the platform's unique features, such as the strain grading system and terpene tracking.

## 5. Success Criteria
The success of this research plan will be measured by its ability to directly inform the subsequent strategic documents. The insights gathered must provide clear, evidence-based recommendations for the website's quality assurance process, the Instagram content strategy, the codebase management workflow, and the post-launch analytics framework. Ultimately, the research must validate StrainScoutMD's value proposition as the most accurate and user-friendly cannabis price comparison tool in Maryland.
